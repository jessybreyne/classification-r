JAKOB BARDRAM'S HOMEPAGE

 

 

Resumé

I work as a research manager for the Danish Centre for Pervasive Healthcare at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Aarhus. The Centre is located in the IT City of Katrinebjerg and is closely associated with the Centre for Pervasive Computing.

I also teach at the Department of Computer Science, and if you are a student a DAIMI and you're interested in participating in this research, feel free to send me an email.

I have been working for 3 years at IBM Denmark as a project manager and IT architect. Currently on leave from IBM.

Member of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).

Research Areas

Pervasive Computer Systems, Object Oriented Software Architecture, Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW); Human-Computer Interaction (HCI); Application of Pervasive Computing Technologies in Healthcare (aka. 'Pervasive Healthcare').
