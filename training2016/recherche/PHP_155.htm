Thanks for Dropping By!  - justindaniels .org

Welcome to Justin Daniels .org. This site has lots of great pictures and things. At least you'll think so if you have any of the same interests as I do.

You would think that after owning and maintaining this site for over 3 years, that by now i would have had something more to write on the home page. That is main reason why you will find no blog here. I find that you already have too much to do to read about me all the time.

However, I do like to show people about the things that I like to do, and about stuff that matters to me. That is my goal with this site. You will get a good sense of what I'm all about. I'll provide links to other pages you might like, since you are checking me out, then maybe you'll find one of those sites useful.

One thing you wont see here however, is anything about my work (except for my resume/portfolio). Since I'm a computer person, I find that there is already so much good computer stuff out there on the net that I don't need to add anything.

So with all that said, enjoy the site. Give a shoutback if you feel like it.

Please link back to me if you have web site!!!!

- Justin Daniels
