Jem (0 / Music)

I’m sitting here waiting for a download to complete listening to the music stream from Jem’s website, because i can’t wait to get the CD.

Jem first came to my attention whilst listening to the O.C. soundtrack (”Just a ride - Jem") many moons ago, gave her 5 stars in iTunes and kinda forgot all about her until i heard her debut single ("They - Jem") on Moyles’ show. And on the basis of hearing those two songs i bought the album, something i never really as it usually takes me a while to really get into an artist enough to buy the album.

Plus, it turns out she’s welsh, we’re great.

Google search never forgets (0 / Life, Work, Smallville)

I often find myself wondering why i bother paying for webspace to host a blog when i don’t really use it, sure it kept me busy designing it but since then i’ve just gotten bored with it, kinda defines what im like really.

So, what have i been upto the last few weeks? I know you’re all dying to know, but it’s not all that interesting, since i work nights (7pm-6am mostly) i rarely get the time to go out and do anything, so this week all my days off have been filled with me watching smallville season 1 again.

I have decided to try and eat a bit more healthily and i need to start walking more, seeing as my job involves me sitting down all night drinking coffee, then i go home and watch smallville all day - which makes me into one big couch potato. I’ve started eating fruit instead of snacks from the vending machine, problem is that fruit never really satisfies me like a big chucky kit-kat does, ho hum.

Don’t spit into the wind (1 / Site, Music)

While buying a few tracks off iTunes (Harry Nilsson - One and Josh Turner - You Don’t Mess Around With Jim), i’ve only just noticed that you can preview the songs, id always assumed that this a was a ‘missing’ feature seeing as there is no preview button. Oh well.

Buns of steel (0 / Smallville)

I’ve just finished watching Unsafe, the latest episode of Smallville to air in the US, and i am so pleased to see Sarah Carter back as Alicia.

Im really enjoying this season of smallville, it’s not as freak-of-the-week as the earlier seasons but it’s not as heavily arced like last season, a happy medium i think.

The whole Lana thing is tedious now though, i have no problem in beleiving that clark is an alien from the planet krypton but the arc about Lana being a decendant of some witch is a bit to much. I do like Lois though.
